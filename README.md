### 🧠 About

I'm a robotics and applied AI engineer with a **Doctor of Engineering** from the University of Bath, focused on the intersection of **reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and vision-language grounding** for real-world robotic systems.

My doctoral and postdoctoral research taught ground robots to perform cinematic camera motion — a hard perception + control problem that turns out to generalise well across robotics domains. I'm now extending that work into **language-conditioned control** (CLIP / SigLIP) and **production-grade agentic systems** (FastAPI, Postgres, Neo4j, Claude API) through my studio, Eight Sprouts.

- 🔬 **Published at IEEE/RSJ IROS 2024** · follow-up submitted to IEEE RA-L
- 🛠 **Currently building** an AI-powered news briefing service with a knowledge-graph-backed agentic retrieval layer
- 🎯 **Interested in** robotics foundation models, VLA systems, sim-to-real learning, and AI-native products
- 📍 Bournemouth, UK · open to London / remote / relocation
- 📬 **Reviewer** for IEEE ICRA 2026

### ⚒️ Featured Projects

> **🚧 Work in progress.** Selected projects below are being prepared for public release over the coming weeks. Watch this space.

#### 📰 Eight Sprouts — AI News Briefing *(in production)*
Agentic news briefing service with payments, Telegram / email delivery, and a conversational query layer.
**Stack:** FastAPI · PostgreSQL · Neo4j (knowledge graph) · Claude API · agentic retrieval (n8n → production rewrite)
**Status:** prototype validated, production launch 2026.

#### 🎥 Language-Conditioned Gimbal Control *(in progress)*
Taking a natural-language prompt → gimbal motion using a VLM-conditioned policy. Built on a Sony A7C II + DJI Ronin RS4 research platform with ROS 2 integration.
**Stack:** ROS 2 · PyTorch · SigLIP · Sony SDK · Ronin SDK

#### 🤖 [ROS_examples](https://github.com/Philori22/ROS_examples)
Basics, motion, OpenCV, localisation, navigation, and SLAM — packaged ROS tutorials and reference implementations.

#### 🧠 [DDPG-aigym](https://github.com/Philori22/DDPG-aigym)
Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient implemented from scratch in PyTorch against OpenAI Gym environments.

#### 🎯 [yolov5_ros1](https://github.com/Philori22/yolov5_ros1)
YOLOv5 object detection integrated as a ROS 1 node.
