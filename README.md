### 🔬 In the lab

#### 🎥 `transformer-gimbal` &nbsp;·&nbsp; *in progress*

> Natural-language-conditioned cinematic camera control on a real gimbal platform.
>
> Ongoing research project on my personal rig (Sony A7C II + DJI Ronin RS4 + ROS 2). Started with CLIP / SigLIP embeddings as a lightweight grounding layer; currently pivoting to a transformer-based policy after finding the embedding-only approach under-conditioned the action distribution. Writing up lessons as I go.

`ROS 2` · `PyTorch` · `Transformers` · `Sony SDK` · `Ronin SDK`
